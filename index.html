Learn How To Cook The Real Brazilian Rice.

Legitimate Brazilian rice should be loose, low in fat and well seasoned. It usually doesn't take strong seasonings, only onion and garlic. Perfect to accompany most recipes!

This Is How You Do A Real Brazilian Beans.

Brazilian beans have a very wide variety of colors. Usually, the most used to make common beans are: black, carioca or red. Like rice, our beans don't use strong spices, just onion, garlic and sometimes bay leaf. As a Brazilian I must say: never put the rice over the beans, people may get mad with you. The rice come first!
